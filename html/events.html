Arriving in Town

Airport to Menger Hotel:
The San Antonio Airport is about 9 miles from the Menger Hotel and other downtown hotels. There is a shuttle service available through Goairportshuttle. They charge $21/person after taxes. For people travelling in a group, we recommend using a taxi service, as a taxi ride from the airport to the hotel is about $30.

Menger Hotel - www.mengerhotel.com
204 Alamo Plaza,
San Antonio, TX 78205
(210) 223-4361

Driving directions from the airport:

  • Take US-281 from E Terminal Dr.
  • Continue on US-281 S for 7 miles
  • Continue onto I-37S/US-281 S.
  • Take exit 141B towards Houston St/The Alamo
  • Continue on Elm St. Drive to E. Crockett St.
  • Take 2nd right onto Bonham
  • Again turn left onto E Crockett St (Destination will be on left)

Ceremony and Reception - Saturday Morning, July 26th

The Wedding ceremony will begin at 11:30am. The Menger Hotel is 0.7 miles from Club Giraud if you want to walk, or you can take a taxi for about $5. There is also a VIA blueline shuttle ($1.25) that leaves from the Alamo area to Club Giraud. (More information about the VIA shuttle service can be found at Via's website) The wedding ceremony and reception will begin at 11:30am. You can probably catch a ride back to your hotel after the reception with other wedding guests.

River Walk - Saturday Evening, July 26th

After the reception, we hope you'll enjoy downtown San Antonio. See the Alamo, the River Walk and visit La Villita. Get a view of the whole city from the Tower of Americas, and have a drink or a snack at the bar to avoid paying for the elevator.

This website offers many other ideas. Nithya and Michael will venture out in the cool evening for a stroll on the River Walk, dinner, and finally mango ice cream at the Menger. You're free to explore on your own or join us.

Bike Ride - Sunday Morning, July 27th

Sunday morning join us for a bike ride to Mission Conception. We’ll be starting at 7:30am to enjoy the cool weather and will be using San Antonio B-Cycle. You can either bring your own helmet or check one out at the Central Hub location in Hemisfair Park during regular business hours.

We will start our 3 mile ride to Mission Conception at the bike “Share station” near the southwest corner of “Hemisfair Plaza” at the intersection of Alamo Street and Cesar Chavez street. You can rent bikes there, or at any “B-Cycle Share Station” you see on the way there. At the Share Station at Mission Conception, we will refuel with snacks and drinks. Interested people may briefly walk around the Mission and also see an interesting travertine outcrop. The Mission asks that we finish by 9:15am when they begin to fill up for their Sunday morning Mass.

We will then bike back to Hemisfair Plaza to return the bikes. We estimate that the trip will cost $18/person.
